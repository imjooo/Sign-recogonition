Our Aim
We want to provide a platform for people who are deaf or hard of hearing to communicate with the rest of the world.


Our Model:
When a user uses sign language to communicate with another person, the pre-trained model uses the recorded gestures to send the desired message to the recipient in the form of text or speech.
